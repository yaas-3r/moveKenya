How to Transport Fragile Items Safely

Protect your valuables with the right packing methods

1. Use High-Quality Packing Supplies

Always use sturdy boxes, bubble wrap, packing peanuts, and cushioning foam to protect breakables during transit.

2. Wrap Items Individually

Each fragile item should be wrapped separately with bubble wrap or soft cloths to prevent contact and collision during movement.

3. Fill Empty Spaces

Use crumpled paper or towels to fill gaps in boxes to prevent shifting and breaking of items inside the boxes.

4. Label Boxes as "Fragile"

Clearly mark all boxes with fragile items. Let your mover know in advance so they can handle them with extra care.
