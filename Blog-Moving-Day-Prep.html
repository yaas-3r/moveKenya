5 Things to Do Before Moving Day

Plan ahead to avoid last-minute stress and confusion

1. Declutter Your Belongings

Take time to go through everything you own. Donate, sell, or dispose of what you don't need. This reduces moving costs and makes unpacking easier.

2. Label Everything Clearly

Use color-coded labels or bold markers to identify which box belongs where. This will save time and reduce the chance of items getting lost.

3. Pack an Essentials Box

Include things like toiletries, snacks, chargers, a change of clothes, and important documents. Keep it accessible during the move.

4. Confirm Details with Your Mover

Contact your moving company (or confirm through moveKenya) to ensure timing, location, and vehicle preferences are accurate.

5. Prepare Your New Home

Ensure your destination is clean, has electricity/water, and is accessible for the moving vehicle. This avoids any last-minute inconvenience.
