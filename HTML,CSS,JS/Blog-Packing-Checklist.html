Checklist: What to Pack First and Last

Strategize your packing order to save time and reduce stress

Pack These First:

  • Non-essentials: Books, seasonal clothes, decorations
  • Storage items: Things you rarely use like tools, spare electronics
  • Backup kitchenware: Extra plates or utensils

Pack These Last:

  • Daily use items: Toiletries, chargers, essential clothes
  • Valuables: Jewelry, passports, cash
  • Cleaning supplies: For last-minute touch-ups

Label boxes accordingly and keep the last-minute ones separate in a designated "Open First" area. This helps you settle faster in your new home.
