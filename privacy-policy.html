Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your information at moveKenya

1. Introduction

At moveKenya, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information such as your name, phone number, email address, pickup and drop-off locations, and any additional notes you provide during the booking process.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us coordinate your move, match you with appropriate moving partners, provide real-time updates, and deliver customer support. We may also use your data for service improvements and communication.

4. Data Sharing

We only share your information with trusted moving partners for the sole purpose of fulfilling your request. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Security

We implement strict security protocols to safeguard your personal data, including HTTPS encryption, secure servers, and limited access controls.

6. Cookies

Our site may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if preferred.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. You can contact us directly for assistance with your data.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect new services or legal requirements. We encourage you to review it regularly.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your data, contact us at privacy@movekenya.co.ke.
