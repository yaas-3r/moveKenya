Frequently Asked Questions

Your top questions about moving answered by our experts

How do I book a mover through moveKenya?

Simply visit our Booking page, select the service, enter your details, and receive instant recommendations with pricing. Confirm and you're done!

Are the movers verified?

Yes. All movers on moveKenya are verified, reviewed, and rated by past customers. We only work with trusted professionals.

Can I move pets or fragile items?

Absolutely. We offer specialized services for pets, fragile belongings, hazardous items, and even office relocations.

What payment methods are supported?

We support M-Pesa, card payments, and mobile banking. Payment is made securely through our platform.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

No problem. You can cancel or reschedule via your dashboard or contact support 24/7.

Do you offer same-day moving services?

Yes, subject to availability. It's always best to book in advance, but we do handle urgent moves when possible.

Is my data safe with moveKenya?

Yes. We follow strict data privacy practices and never share your personal information with third parties without consent.

Can I track the moving vehicle?

Yes. All moves come with live GPS tracking so you always know where your belongings are.

Do you help with packing and unpacking?

Yes, you can request packing and unpacking assistance during booking. Charges may vary based on the number of items.

Can I customize my move with extra items?

Yes. For home and office moves, you can add bulky or special items like safes or pianos during the customization stage.
